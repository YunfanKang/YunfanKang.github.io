Yunfan Kang is a Visiting Research Scientist at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), working at the CyberGIS Center for Advanced Digital and Spatial Studies. He earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of California, Riverside (UCR), with a research focus on spatial analysis and big spatial data management. His current work centers on geospatial GeoAI and generative AI, and he is the lead developer of the I-GUIDE platform's user environment. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from The University of Hong Kong and has received multiple honors and awards for his academic excellence and research contributions.
