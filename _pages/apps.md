Interactive web apps I've built and deployed — all open-source and running right in your browser.

## CardPointer
*Find the best credit card for every purchase.*

A free, offline-capable progressive web app (PWA) that compares the cards in your wallet and tells you which one earns the most rewards for each shopping category.

[Open App](https://yunfankang.github.io/cardpointer/){: .btn .btn--info} [Source](https://github.com/YunfanKang/cardpointer){: .btn .btn--inverse}

## Champaign County Crime Map
*Interactive, self-updating crime map for Champaign County (2025–2026).*

Visualize and analyze local crime incidents from METCAD data — search locations, filter by time period and crime type, and plan routes with safety scores.

[Open App](https://yunfankang.github.io/champaign-crime-map/){: .btn .btn--info} [Source](https://github.com/YunfanKang/champaign-crime-map){: .btn .btn--inverse}

## Princeton University Crime Map
*Campus crime map for Princeton University (SpotCrime, Spring 2026).*

An interactive map of crime incidents at and around Princeton University — filter by type, location, and time, with safety-scored route planning.

[Open App](https://yunfankang.github.io/princeton-crime-map/){: .btn .btn--info} [Source](https://github.com/YunfanKang/princeton-crime-map){: .btn .btn--inverse}

## GasPred
*Should you fill up now or wait?*

A gas-price forecasting app that uses machine learning on historical energy-market data, refinery metrics, and crude-oil futures to predict U.S. gasoline prices.

[Open App](https://yunfankang.github.io/gaspred/){: .btn .btn--info} [Source](https://github.com/YunfanKang/gaspred){: .btn .btn--inverse}
